Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

We primarily ship within Canada and to the United States. International shipping may be available on a case-by-case basis depending on destination country regulations. Contact us for specific details about your location.
Shipping costs vary by location and time of year. Within Canada, overnight shipping typically ranges from $45-$85 CAD. US shipping ranges from $60-$120 USD. We use professional reptile shipping services to ensure safe, temperature-controlled transport. Contact us with your location for an exact quote.
All shipments include professional insulated packaging, heat or cold packs as needed, and overnight delivery. Animals are packaged in secure, breathable containers with appropriate moisture. You'll receive tracking information and we coordinate delivery times to ensure someone is available to receive the package.
All animals come with complete lineage information, a care guide specific to the species, feeding records, and our live arrival guarantee. We're also available for ongoing support and questions after your purchase.
We can arrange payment plans for animals over $300 on a case-by-case basis. Typically, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to hold an animal, with the balance due before shipping. Contact us to discuss options.
Local pickup may be available by appointment for serious buyers. We're located in Canada. Contact us to discuss scheduling and location details.
We guarantee live arrival for all shipped animals. If an issue occurs during transit, you must notify us within 2 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. We also offer a 7-day health guarantee covering any pre-existing conditions. Animals must be housed according to our care guidelines for the guarantee to be valid.
Our Gargoyle Geckos are raised on Repashy Crested Gecko Diet and Pangea as their primary food source. They're also offered appropriately-sized insects (crickets, dubia roaches) as supplemental protein. We feed our geckos every other day and ensure fresh water is always available.
Gargoyle Geckos thrive at room temperature (68-78°F / 20-26°C) with no special heating required in most homes. Humidity should be maintained at 50-70%, achieved through daily misting and a moisture-retaining substrate. They don't require UVB lighting but benefit from a natural day/night cycle.
Yes! Gargoyle Geckos are excellent beginner reptiles. They have simple care requirements, tolerate handling well once acclimated, and have long lifespans (15-20 years). Their room-temperature requirements mean no complex heating setups. We provide a comprehensive care guide with every purchase.
The "Bacon Line" is our signature Gargoyle Gecko breeding project focusing on high-contrast red and orange striping. Named for the resemblance to crispy bacon, these geckos feature bold, saturated colors with strong structural patterns. We've been selectively breeding this line for several generations to consistently produce exceptional offspring.
We occasionally have proven or compatible pairs available. Contact us with your breeding goals and we can help match animals that complement each other genetically. We're happy to share lineage information to help you make informed breeding decisions.
Our breeding season typically runs from spring through fall. Hatchlings are usually available from late spring through early winter. Follow us on Instagram for updates on clutches and expected hatch dates. You can also join our email list to be notified when new animals become available.
